Put your custom yasnippets here.
Take a look at the yasnippet documentation: http://capitaomorte.github.com/yasnippet/

Create a directory with the mode-name:

~/.emacs.d/
|
`-- snippets/
    |
    |-- python-mode/
    |   |-- time.yasnippet
    |   `-- pprint
    |
    `-- ruby-mode/
        `-- email.yasnippet
